Galactic Devil Magic Wars

A Science Fiction Plot
by Hayden Drysdale

A long, long time ago in a devil, devil galaxy...

After leaving the evil planet Hell, a group of Satanic-Worship fly toward a distant speck. The speck gradually resolves into a blood, space Death.

Civil war strikes the galaxy, which is ruled by first last, a death Satan capable of Satanic-Worship and even Satanic-Curse.

Terrified, a murder Hellanic known as Pete Fish flees the Empire, with his protector, Hayden Drysdale.

They head for Hell-Town on the planet Satanic-Hell. When they finally arrive, a fight breaks out. Drysdale uses his devil Magic to defend Pete.

Drysdale and Hellanic Pete decide it's time to leave Satanic-Hell and steal an Alien to shoot their way out.

They encounter a tribe of Satan. Drysdale is attacked and the Hellanic is captured by the Satan and taken back to Hell-Town.

Drysdale must fight to save Hellanic Pete but when he accidentally unearths a kill Person, the entire future of the devil, evil galaxy is at stake.
